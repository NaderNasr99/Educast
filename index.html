Who are we?

Educast is a non-profit organization that provides teachers with Professional Development opportunities to help them deepen and strengthen their knowledge and get exposed to different expertise from the same or different teaching contexts.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make education affordable and obtainable for people from all backgrounds. Our effort is to always bring forward the latest methodologies of teaching for every teacher. We want to ensure that they are equipped with the knowledge of established as well as up and coming techniques of teaching. The goal is to make the educator proficient and globally viable.
